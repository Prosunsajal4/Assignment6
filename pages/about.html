About Green Earth

We're on a mission to restore our planet, one tree at a time. Since our founding, we've planted over 500,000 trees across 30+ countries.

Our Story

Green Earth was founded in 2020 by a group of environmental scientists and activists who recognized the urgent need to combat climate change through large-scale reforestation.

What started as a small community project has grown into a global movement, involving thousands of volunteers, corporate partners, and local communities in our mission to plant 1 million trees by 2030.

Our Vision

A world where every community has access to green spaces, clean air, and sustainable ecosystems that support both human and wildlife populations.
